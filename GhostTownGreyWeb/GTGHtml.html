Ghost Town Grey (GTG) is a six-piece metalcore band based out of Beaverton/Portland, OR. Our musical roots are firmly planted in making heavy music with sing-along melodies, intense "chuggy" rhythms, and a message that promotes positivity. After being selected by Sumerian Records to compete in the second round of Headbang for the Highway's "Battle for the All-Star Tour," we are moving forward with a new vision with even bigger goals to take us to the next level.
